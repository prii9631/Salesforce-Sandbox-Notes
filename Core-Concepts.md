**Records,Fields and Objects**

**OBJECTS = REAL WORLD ENTITIES**

An object is simply a real-world thing or entity that exists around us. It could be a person, animal, vehicle, building, or any physical or conceptual thing.

**FIELDS = ATTRIBUTES/PROPERTIES**

Fields are the characteristics, properties, or attributes that describe that real-world entity. They tell us specific details about the object.

**RECORDS = COMPLETE DESCRIPTION**

A record is the complete collection of all those attributes for one specific object.

**REAL-LIFE EXAMPLES**

EXAMPLE 1: CAR (OBJECT)

**Attributes/Fields:**

* Brand (Toyota, Honda, BMW)
* Model (Camry, Civic, X5)
* Year (2020, 2023)
* Color (Red, Blue, Black)
* Engine Size (2.0L, 3.5L)
* Number of Doors (2, 4)
* Price ($25,000, $45,000)

**EXAMPLE 2: PERSON (OBJECT)**

**Attributes/Fields:**

* Name (John Smith, Sarah Johnson)
* Age (25, 34)
* Height (5'8", 6'2")
* Weight (150 lbs, 180 lbs)
* Eye Color (Brown, Blue)
* Address (123 Main St, 456 Oak Ave)
* Phone Number (555-1234, 555-5678)

**EXAMPLE 3: BOOK (OBJECT)**

**Attributes/Fields:**

* Title (Harry Potter, To Kill a Mockingbird)
* Author (J.K. Rowling, Harper Lee)
* Pages (400, 281)
* Genre (Fantasy, Fiction)
* Would you like me to extract text from another image?
